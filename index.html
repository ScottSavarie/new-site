  • Profile

    I’m a Product Designer from Canada working mostly on web and native applications. Lately I’ve been writing more and more front-end code and couldn’t be happier with how this influences design decisions and workflow. I’ve been lucky enough to work on projects for companies such as Red Bull and Oracle, as well as various young start ups and studios. Currently I’m making cool stuff at Facebook and learning a ton while I'm at it. Aside from work, I’m usually playing in bands, building things with wood, and stuffing my face with burritos.

  • Experience

    Since 2009 I’ve worked with various companies in Canada, Germany, and The United States. I spent some time working with a few start ups and small companies such as Projecturf, Fileboard, and Appuous and at one point launched my own Mac application called Drip. I spent one and a half years in Berlin working with some amazing people at Edenspiekermann. I’m a graduate of Graphic Design from Cambrian College of Art and Design.
